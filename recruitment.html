Information for Potential Researchers and PhD Students

I am currently working on a joint project for Data Fusion and Machine Learning for Geothermal Target Exploration and Characterization. See more details at the Geothermal project site. There are tons of interesting machine learning problems related to this project. We have real problems with real data. Drop me an email if you are interested.

NICTA is also hiring researchers at all levels. Please see here.

If you are interested in doing a PhD in probabilistic machine learning at the Australian National University (ANU) under my supervision please first read the Graduate Research Info. If you pass the self assesment, feel free to contact me via email attaching the pre-application Expression of Interest Form. Do not forget to attach your CV and a copy of your academic transcripts. (All documents must be in pdf format.)

You must have a strong math background and good programming skills. I will support your application as long as (a) you are indeed an outstanding student and (b) your project proposal  is aligned with my research interest (preferably in the areas of graphical models, nonparametric Bayesian methods, Gaussian processes, active learning of user preferences, Bayesian recommender systems).

You will have the opportunity to apply for scholarships but you will need to start your application well before 31st August (preferably 2 months before).
